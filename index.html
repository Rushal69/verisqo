ABOUT US

Versiqo is a personal operating system designed to help students manage the side of life that academics don't cover. Built by a student, for students, it brings together daily planning, habit tracking, mental wellness, budgeting, and career tools — all in one clean, minimalist system. Versiqo, our core product, was created to solve the real problems students face: missed deadlines, burnout, overspending, disorganization, and lack of direction. Every workspace is crafted to be useful, aesthetic, and ready to use — no setup, no fluff. Versiqo helps you stay focused, balanced, and in control of your student life, with tools you'll actually enjoy opening every day.
